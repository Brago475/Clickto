# Clickto

**Record. Replay. Repeat.**

A free, simple auto-clicker for macOS and Windows. Record a sequence of mouse
clicks once, then replay it at any speed, on a loop, with hotkeys to pause and
stop.

No account. No subscription. No ads.

---

## Download

Grab the latest version from the [Releases page](https://github.com/Brago475/Clickto/releases/latest).

- **macOS** — download `Clickto.dmg`, open it, and drag Clickto to Applications.
- **Windows** — download `Clickto-Setup-1.1.0.exe` and run the installer.

---

## Features

- Record and replay mouse clicks with their timing
- Repeat a set number of times or loop forever
- Adjustable playback speed and a seamless-loop option
- Save, load, and delete click patterns
- Two configurable hotkeys (pause/resume and panic stop)
- 3-2-1 countdown before playback
- Dark and light themes

---

## How to use

1. **Set your hotkeys** (optional) — pick keys for Stop and Pause/Resume.
2. **Record** — click Record, then click the spots you want, in order. Press
   your Stop key (or Stop Rec) to finish.
3. **Choose** how it runs — a repeat count (or Forever) and a speed.
4. **Start** — a short countdown gives you time to move your hands away, then
   it replays your clicks.
5. **Control it live** — pause/resume or stop instantly with your hotkeys.

Save a pattern with a name, load it later, or delete ones you no longer need.

## First launch

**macOS** — grant Accessibility and Input Monitoring in
*System Settings → Privacy & Security*, then reopen Clickto. Since the app is
unsigned, right-click it and choose **Open** the first time.

**Windows** — SmartScreen may warn on an unsigned app. Click **More info →
Run anyway**.
